A man who was coming back from Bengaluru airport had a unique experience as he was the only passenger on a Bengaluru Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC) bus. Hariharen (@thisishariharen), from Tamil Nadu’s Coimbatore, took to X to share a selfie with the driver and conductor of the BMTC bus. He said these two men operated the bus only for him just to stick to the timings. He said they gave him good company and a safe ride back home. “Was coming back from the airport, These 2 gentlemen operated the bus just for me, just to keep up the timings.
